About

About Me

I am a computational biologist with a Ph.D. in Bioinformatics. My academic work connects bioinformatics, molecular biology, cheminformatics, deep generative modeling, and computational drug design. My research focuses on de novo molecule design for specific targets, molecular generation, bioactivity-oriented modeling, and AI-driven methods for therapeutic candidate discovery.
