About

About Me

I work at the intersection of bioinformatics, cheminformatics, artificial intelligence, and drug discovery. My research explores how generative models, molecular representations, and deep learning architectures can accelerate the design, optimization, and prioritization of drug-like molecules.

Scientific Dimensions

Research Interests

01 // MOLECULAR GENERATION

Generative AI for Molecules

Designing novel drug-like molecules using deep generative models, SMILES-based architectures, transformers, and representation learning.

02 // DRUG DESIGN

Computational Drug Design

Applying machine learning, cheminformatics, docking analysis, and predictive modeling to support rational therapeutic discovery.

03 // BIOINFORMATICS

AI for Biomedical Discovery

Developing computational frameworks for bioactivity prediction, multi-target design, low-data drug discovery, and biomedical decision-making.
