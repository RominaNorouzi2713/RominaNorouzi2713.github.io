About Romina Norouzi

Romina Norouzi is a bioinformatics researcher with a Ph.D. in Bioinformatics. Her work focuses on artificial intelligence for biomedical data, computational drug design, molecular biology, and data-driven therapeutic discovery.

Research Profile

She is particularly interested in how AI models can learn from biomedical and molecular data to generate, evaluate, and prioritize meaningful candidate molecules. Her background also includes molecular genetics, cell and molecular biology, multiomics data analysis, genome and transcriptome assembly, and experimental molecular biology techniques.
