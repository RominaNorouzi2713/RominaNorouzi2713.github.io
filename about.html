About Romina Norouzi

Romina Norouzi is a bioinformatics researcher with a Ph.D. in Bioinformatics. She completed her doctoral studies from 2021 to 2026 in the Department of Bioinformatics at the Institute of Biochemistry and Biophysics, University of Tehran, within the Laboratory of Bioinformatics and Drug Design.

Research Profile

Her research centers on artificial intelligence for biomedical data, computational drug design, molecular biology, and data-driven therapeutic discovery. She is particularly interested in how AI models can learn from biomedical and molecular data to generate, evaluate, and prioritize meaningful candidate molecules.
